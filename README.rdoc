= Xray

In your Gemfile:

gem 'xray', path: '...wherever you cloned to...'

In your routes.br:

mount Xray::Engine => '/xray'

Then run your app.  Go to localhost:3000/xray to inspect your database.

Reloads the browser page automatically.  You might want to turn that off.
